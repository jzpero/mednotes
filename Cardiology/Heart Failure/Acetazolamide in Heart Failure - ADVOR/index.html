Background

  • ineffective diuresis and residual volume overload is common and associated with poor outcomes
  • Sequential nephron blockade may be more effective than loop monotherapy
    • Limited evidence for this
    • Previous small trial with indapamide
    • 3T trial (2020) - Patients were randomized to oral metolazone, IV chlorothiazide, or tolvaptan therapy.
    • JACC 2019

Acetazolamide

  • MOA
    • CA inhibitor
    • Reduces prox tubular Na reabsorption
    • Increases urinary sodium excretion
